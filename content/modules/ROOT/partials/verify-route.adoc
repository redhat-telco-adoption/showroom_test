Verify that the application route is accessible and returning a successful response:

[source,bash]
----
curl -s -o /dev/null -w "%{http_code}\n" https://<route-hostname>
----

Expected output:

----
200
----

A `200` response confirms the application is running and reachable. A `503` typically means the service has no healthy pods to route traffic to.

To get the route hostname:

[source,bash]
----
oc get route <route-name> -o jsonpath='{.spec.host}'
----
